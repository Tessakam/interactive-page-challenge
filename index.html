BeCode is Belgium’s first coding and music festival: 2 days and 2 stages, with the biggest headliners, young talents, raging rockers, incredible singer-songwriters, new names, and the coolest beats around.

In 2021, BeCode Festival will take place between Saterday 3 July and Sunday 4 July.
The combination of coding workshops, hackathons and world-class music makes BeCode Festival a unique experience!

    1. How to get there?
      The BeCode festival is centrally located in the Warandepark in Brussels (Belgium).
      Public transport or traveling by bike are the most practical and convenient ways of getting to BeCode Festival.
      By train: Your festival ticket includes 1 free and personal train ticket for a return journey in 2nd class from a Belgian departure station to BeCode festival.
